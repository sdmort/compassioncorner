My Therapeutic Approach

Strong Therapeutic Relationship

At Compassion Corner Counseling, I believe in building a strong therapeutic relationship based on trust and understanding. I place a strong emphasis on creating a safe and nonjudgmental space where you can explore your thoughts, emotions, and experiences openly.

Individualized and Holistic

My approach is centered around understanding your individual needs and collaborating with you to develop tailored strategies that support your unique goals. I offer a flexible and creative therapeutic approach that integrates various modalities including Compassion-Focused Therapy, Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (MCBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and more. This eclectic approach allows me to adapt to your specific needs and preferences, ensuring that our sessions are effective and relevant to your personal journey.

Empowering You Towards Growth!

My goal is to empower you to overcome challenges, cultivate self-compassion, and achieve greater peace and fulfillment in your life. Whether you're navigating self-esteem and identity issues, exploring nontraditional relationships like polyamory/ethical nonmonogamy, or seeking support for your children and family dynamics, I provide insightful guidance and practical tools to support your growth and well-being.
